## About This Project

Selected pages from [www.radai.com](https://www.radai.com), the public-facing website for Rad AI — a healthcare AI company delivering AI-powered radiology workflow automation and reporting solutions for health systems, hospitals, and radiology groups.

**Project Scope:** These pages represent the areas of the site where I had the deepest involvement during my tenure as Senior Marketing Communications Manager. My role spanned UI/UX, branding, copywriting, imagery and creative direction, interactivity, layout, and ongoing content management via Webflow CMS. I also collaborated with external developers to implement technical interactive elements and custom scripts, and partnered with vendors on photography and videography production.
